# Series Sequencing and Cadence

## Audience
Growth-stage startup PMs.

## Cadence
- 2 articles/week for 7 weeks.
- 1 capstone week for the final article.

## Release Plan
- Week 1: Articles 01-02
- Week 2: Articles 03-04
- Week 3: Articles 05-06
- Week 4: Articles 07-08
- Week 5: Articles 09-10
- Week 6: Articles 11-12
- Week 7: Articles 13-14
- Week 8: Article 15

## Editorial Consistency Rules
- One central thesis per article.
- Max two frameworks per article.
- Include one "apply Monday morning" block.
- Include one explicit failure mode and tradeoff.
- Cross-link to one prior or next article.

## Drafting Workflow (per article)
1. 20 min thesis + angle lock.
2. 35 min first draft.
3. 20 min structural edit.
4. 15 min credibility pass.
